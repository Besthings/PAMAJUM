ABOUT PAMAJUM

What is blind box?

"BLIND BOX" is a type of sealed packaging that keeps what's inside a mystery until you open it. If you order a "Set of 12 Boxes", you will receive a set of 12 figures in 12 different designs. Besides "basic" figures, some figures are rarer to find, which we call "SECRET".

Why should you buy
Blind Boxes from our store?

"Why shop with us? At Blind Box, every box is a carefully curated surprise filled with high-quality items, offering great value, excitement, and exclusive products that you won’t find anywhere else, all delivered quickly and with care.".

Will I receive 100% authentic products?

"We guarantee that any item you order, whether it's a doll, keychain, figure, etc., will be 100% authentic. Plus, we offer free shipping when you purchase a set of 6 or 12 items, and our blind boxes are carefully wrapped with high-quality bubble wrap.".
